Observations are raw insights extracted from your coding sessions before they've been processed. When a session ends, ai-memory analyzes the conversation and extracts notable observations — things like decisions you made, patterns you follow, or preferences you expressed. Pending observations haven't been synthesized into memories yet. Synthesized observations have already been incorporated into one or more memories. Observations that get skipped too many times are automatically cleaned up.

## Deleting Observations

Click the trash icon on any observation card to delete it. You can also delete all observations for a project by hovering over a project header in the observation sidebar and clicking its delete button.
